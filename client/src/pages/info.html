About TalkBridge

TalkBridge is an AI-powered communication platform built to bridge the gap between sign language users and spoken language users.

Our Smart Camera uses a Machine Learning model for real-time Sign ↔ Speech translation during video calls, making conversations more natural, inclusive, and accessible.

We are focused on creating technology that enables equal communication opportunities for everyone, regardless of hearing or speech limitations.

Key Features

  • Real-time Sign to Speech conversion
  • Real-time Speech to Sign support
  • AI-enhanced video calling experience
  • Simple, modern, and accessible interface
